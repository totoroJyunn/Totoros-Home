# Totoros-Home
Try to make sure that every action you take reduces the entropy of this space.
In other words, leave the place more orderly and pleasant than you found it.

About inviting others to stay(for people who have stayed here before):
If you want to invite someone to stay here, you may decide on your own and give them the address.
However, do not allow them to pass the address on to others.
Only people who have stayed here before may invite new guests.
